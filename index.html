About Riwaq Labs
The riwaq was the colonnaded portico of the great mosques — the shaded arcade where scholars gathered, debated, and taught. We carry that spirit into the digital age.

Riwaq al-Samt is an independent Islamic applications studio building free digital tools for Muslims worldwide. We do not compromise on quality, and we ask nothing in return.

Al-Samt — the zenith — is the point directly above you, the celestial reference from which Islamic astronomers calculated prayer times and Qibla direction. The word itself gave English the word "azimuth." It is the most Islamic of coordinates.
