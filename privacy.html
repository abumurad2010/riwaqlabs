Privacy Policy

Mawaqit | مواقيت — Developed by Riwaq Labs

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Mawaqit is developed by Riwaq Labs. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. We are fully committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data: The app requests access to your device location solely to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your city. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

User Preferences: Settings such as your chosen prayer calculation method, language, theme, and notification preferences are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

No Account Required: The app does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Location data to external servers
  • Any data to external servers whatsoever

4. Permissions

Location (optional): Used only to auto-detect your city for prayer time calculation. You may manually enter your city and deny this permission entirely.

Notifications (optional): Used to deliver Adhan and Athkar reminders at times you configure. You may disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or social media SDKs.

The app uses the Nominatim geocoding service (openstreetmap.org) only when you perform a manual city search. No personal data is sent — only the city name you type.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
